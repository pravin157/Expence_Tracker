### Setting Your Budget Limits

#### Monthly Limit:
1. Scroll to "Monthly Limit" section

#### Yearly Limit:
1. Scroll to "Yearly Limit" section
2. Enter your target yearly spending amount
3. System shows current year's spending vs limit
4. Tap "Save Limit"

### Budget Features:

**Real-time Tracking**:
- See exactly how much you've spent against your limit
- Visual progress indicator with percentage
- Green = Within budget | Red = Over budget

**Budget Recommendations**:
- 50/30/20 Rule: 50% needs, 30% wants, 20% savings
- Set Conservative Limits: Start low and adjust as needed
- Review Monthly: Check spending and adjust limits regularly

**Budget Alerts**:
- Red alert cards appear when you exceed limits
- Shows how much over you are
- Helps you quickly identify budget breaches
